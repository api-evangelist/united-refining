# United Refining Company (united-refining)

United Refining Company is an independent, integrated petroleum company headquartered in Warren, Pennsylvania. The company owns and operates a petroleum refinery and a network of retail gasoline stations and convenience stores under the Kwik Fill and Country Fair brands across the northeastern United States. United Refining does not offer publicly documented developer APIs or digital integration services.
